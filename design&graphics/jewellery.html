    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Do you want to work on jewellery for a living? If yes, jewellery designing is an attractive field for you. Jewellery is the amalgamation of the creative skills of a designer and the accuracy of skilled artisans. The jewellery doesn't need to be made of precious stone; ornaments worn by the early humans can also be categorized as jewellery. Earlier artisans were also the designers of the jewellery and the market was in the hands of few but now the field of jewellery making has opened its doors to everyone.

    Jewellery designing can be proven as a flourishing career if the candidate is creative enough. In the Indian economy, jewelry is a key source of investment and has a significant economic impact. Jewelry designers create wearable works of art using precious metals, diamonds, beads, and gemstones.

    Description

    Making jewellery does not limit itself to designing but it progresses to manufacturing and then trade. Although most of the professionals stick to the designing part it is important to have knowledge of the stone and their cutting, polishing and testing. The knowledge of stone is necessary for the designer to envision the capacity, lustre and life of the stone. They need to make a practical design to ensure that the stone fits in the design. Computers play a significant role in today’s market. Designers must update themselves with the newly available software.

    Skills You Need

    • Creativity Skills: It is the main essence of the job. Only highly creative people can sustain this job.
    • Artistic Skills: Jewellery designing is about applying art to accessories thus, it requires artistic thinking.
    • Communication Skills: Designers are in direct contact with the clients. They need to understand the demands of the clients to deliver the expected product.
    • Drawing Skills: Designers first make the sketches before working on the project.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: BSc. Fashion Designing / B.Des. Accessory Design / B.Des. Jewellery Retail and Export Management / Diploma in Jewellery Designing.
    • Post Graduation: Masters in Fashion Design / M.Des. Accessory Design / Advanced Diploma in Jewellery Designing.
