    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Have you always had an interest in clothes and accessories? If yes, then fashion designing is a potential career option for you. Fashion design is one of the most popular creative courses among students.

    Fashion design is a humanities specialisation that encompasses the vast range of fabrics, designs, patterns, colours, and trends that characterize a given era's fashion. The art of applying style, aesthetics, and natural beauty to clothing and accessories is known as fashion design. Within a decade, the apparel industry has advanced by leaps and bounds. Manufacturers employ their unique designs. The essence of a designer's role is to interpret trends; however, there is variation within this that is determined by the buying and merchandising approach, as well as product quality; for example, budget retailers will use inexpensive fabrics to interpret trends, whereas high-end retailers will use the best available fabrics.

    Description

    Designers study fashion trends and translate them for their target audience. The purchasing and merchandising strategy, as well as product quality, decide the difference within this; for example, budget retailers will use cheap fabrics to interpret patterns, while high-end retailers will use the best available fabrics. Fashion designers generally work on wearable garments and try to make them aesthetically pleasing. Designers work within a wide range of materials, colours, patterns and styles, and choose the best option according to the client’s taste. Designers study fashion trends and interpret them for their target audience.

    Skills You Need

    • Creativity Skills: It is the main essence of the job. Only highly creative people can sustain this job.
    • Artistic Skills: Fashion designing is about applying art to fabrics thus, it requires artistic thinking.
    • Competition Skills: Fashion designing is a very competitive field as it is ever-changing and there is the constant addition of new talent in the field. Thus, to sustain in the field, one has to be very competitive.
    • Communication Skills: Designers are in direct contact with the clients. They need to understand the demands of the clients to deliver the expected product.
    • Drawing Skills: Designers first make the sketches before working on the project.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: Bachelor of Science in Fashion Design / Bachelor of Design (Fashion Design).
    • Post Graduation: Master of Science in Fashion Design / Master of Design (Fashion Design).

    Pros

    • Satisfaction and sense of achievement as one gets to be creative.
    • Travel opportunities to meet potential customers or for business meetings.
    • Higher pay possibilities after covering the invested amount.

    Cons

    • Tight deadlines as the clients expect delivery on time and the work depends on clients which restrict the creative ability of the designer.
    • High competition because of the incentives in this fields.
    • Need a lot of time, patience and hard work to sustain and create a name for themselves in the field.
