    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Haven't we all been amazed when we saw wild animals on TV? If you could never get over your fascination for wild animals and everything else to do with them, then zoology is probably an apt choice for you. Zoology deals with the study of the animal kingdom. Zoology studies the behaviour, characteristics, evolutionary trends of the different species of animals. It also studies the changing environment and its impact on these species and vice versa.

    Zoology helps us study animals and their habitat from a closer lens. Those people who specialise in this field are known as zoologists. The study of animals has largely moved on to deal with form and function, adaptations, relationships between groups, behaviour and ecology.

    Description

    The career of a zoologist mainly includes a lot of responsibility towards the animal kingdom. Whatever work a zoologist does is to keep us informed about the ways in which the animal kingdom works, thus increasing our awareness. And hopefully, we can live in harmony with them by using the knowledge zoologists give to our world. Zoologists would have to have a great amount of organic knowledge in order to push themselves in this career. They might also have to travel a lot in case they want to study a particular species in a specific region. Systematics is the study of the diversification of living forms, the evolutionary history of a group is known as its phylogeny.

    Skills You Need

    • Determination Skills:The syllabus is vast and sometimes crushing. There is a lot of hard work to be done and social life may come to a standstill.
    • Research Skills: Naturally one would have to be good at research as well to understand new problems, their potential solutions and inventions.
    • Analytical Skills: You would have to have that drive to push yourself in this career in order to achieve highest potential and satisfaction.
    • Reading Skills: You need to be aware of upcoming findings and studies conducted and keep up with the recent developments in this field.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science as the main stream.
    • Graduation: Bachelor of Science in Zoology / Bachelor of Science in Biotechnology,Zoology / Bachelor of Science in Zoology,Chemistry.
    • Post Graduation: Masters of Science in Marine Zoology / Medical Microbiology
