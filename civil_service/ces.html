    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Have you ever been intrigued by the import and export of goods? Then, customs and excise would be a great field for you! We have all had objects ordered or seen being ordered from outside our country.

    Have you ever wondered how people deal with the customs of these objects? Customs, Goods & Services Tax, Central Excise, Service Tax, and Narcotics are all administered by the Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs, which is India's main national agency. Customs is a government entity or agency in charge of collecting customs and controlling the movement of commodities into and out of a country, including animals, transportation, personal effects, and dangerous materials. But once this exam has been cleared, there’s amazing job security, constant salary and amazing privileges.

    Description

    A Customs Officer is an agent who, on behalf of the government, enforces custom law. Customs Officers have to deal with the prevention of illegal goods such as drugs, tobacco, alcohol. They have to have a hawkeye to prevent smuggling in India. They are placed at International airports or ports so that they can govern the import and export of goods. They work in groups to prevent such fraudulent activities. The path to being a customs officer is very tough. The candidate has to prepare and give an exam (UPSC) for this job. Not only is its course tough, but also there are many phases to it.

    Skills You Need

    • Decision Making Skills: One often has to make difficult decisions regarding what the best step to take is after assessing risks, making decision-making skills important.
    • Teamwork Skills: In this profession, one is often working with teams in analysing information and assessing options, making teamwork very important.
    • Communication Skills: One needs to be a very good communicator in order to work with teams of such skilful people.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: Bachelor's in any stream
    • Post Graduation: UPSC Entrance Exams.

    Job Opportunities

    • Inspector Of Customs And Central Excise.
    • Preventive Officer
    • Examiner Of Indian Customs

    Pros

    • It is an extremely high-paying job.
    • Job security in this field is relatively high.
    • This is a very interesting field.

    Cons

    • The way to this career is very arduous.
    • The selection procedure is quite tough.
    • You have to sacrifice a lot of your life to study for this job.
