    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Do you want to design and create a product? If yes, then product designing is the favoured career option for you. The process of product design entails imagining and producing mass-produced products. The concept includes both the physical and functional characteristics that goods should have.

    It's an observational technique that uses a problem-solving approach to enhance the end-quality user's life and interactions with the environment. Certain experts, such as engineers and marketers, collaborate with product designers. Although they are not in control of the product's strictly mechanical and technical elements, they are concerned with usability. Understanding technology, production methods and materials is necessary as the designers use 3-D models, drawings and computer designs to express their ideas.

    Description

    Product designers use their design experience and technological expertise to enhance the functionality and appearance of existing products. Designers also work to reduce the product's expense by rethinking the development. Designers can not only work on existing products, but they can also work on the development of a completely new product. Product designers work closely with colleagues and clients. They even have to work with engineers, model makers, sales and marketing professionals, and other skilled people. Doing all the work designers do, they have to be on a budget and make it to the deadlines.

    Skills You Need

    • Technical Skills: Product designers usually practice on the computer. They need to be familiar with the software and should know the technical specs of the job. They must have IT and CAD skills.
    • Creativity Skills: Any field of designing is a creative field. In such fields theory does not work instead practicality works.
    • Communication Skills: Designers need to coordinate with clients and colleagues. They even need to work with professionals of other fields such as engineers, sales and marketing, etc.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: B. Des in Industrial design / B. Des in Product designing / B. Tech in Mechanical / B. Tech in Automobile / B. Tech in Electrical Engineering
    • Post Graduation: Diploma / M. Des in Product Designing / Diploma / M. Des in Industrial Designing
