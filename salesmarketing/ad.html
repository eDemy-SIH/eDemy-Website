    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Are you always fascinated by different types of advertisements? Then advertising is the appropriate career pathway for you! Advertising is not a new field, everyone has seen advertisement in some form or the other. Advertising is a means of selling, by highlighting the key features of a product or service.

    It is often catchy, to attract the customers. It is one aspect of mass communication, and it involves communicating with a large audience, by the means of your advert. It requires quite a bit of market research, in order to narrow down the targeted demographic. Advertisements, or ads for short, are the real commercial messaging. Advertising's purpose is to contact those who are most inclined to pay for a company's products or services and persuade them to buy.

    Description

    Advertising involves being creative and thinking out of the box. The adverts need to be very catchy and attractive. They need to convey to the customer how your product or service would add value to their lives. Experts in this field also need to possess good communication skills. The advertisements are specifically curated for different groups of people, divided based on factors like gender, age, preferences, geographical location, etc. The advertisements often use means of mass media, such as print media (magazines, newspapers, posters, flyers, billboards), or broadcast media (radio, television).

    Skills You Need

    • Negotiation Skills: As an advertiser you’ll have to negotiate with clients on budgets, timelines, and expectations. You also need to work with a team that works to achieve the same goals as you.
    • Creativity Skills: Creativity is what earns you brownie points in this field, you need to be able to step out and think differently if you want to stand out in the market.
    • Analysing Skills: Analysing the market, the trends and patterns are also extremely important.
    • Communication Skills: One will be primarily interacting with a lot of people on a daily basis.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Science or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: Diploma in Advertising
    • Post Graduation: PG Diploma in Advertising / MA. In Mass Communication.
