    What Makes This Career Worth Pursuing?

    Do you hold a keen interest in finances and stocks? Then chartered market technician might be the right career path for you. The CMT Association certifies that an individual is equipped in conducting technical analysis, via the Chartered Market Technician designation. They look for patterns in prices and past trends in price movement to identify key indicators on the basis of market psychology.

    Professionals who achieve this designation have vast knowledge related to investment risk in portfolio management. Essentially, a CMT designation is a very prestigious designation in the technical analysis field. The Financial Industry Regulatory Authority recognized Chartered Market Technician Designation establishes credibility for those seeking new employment prospects in the financial services industry.

    Description

    The Market Technicians Association has given the duty of checking the proficiency of an analyst in technical analysis, to the CMT Association. Employers who commonly hire CMT-designated analysts include stock brokerage firms, investment banks, mutual fund companies, and so on. To forecast the performance and market action of a stock, a business sector, or the economy, these analysts often employ a holistic approach to securities analysis that combines both fundamental and technical methods. On the basis of their analysis, these analysts make buy-and-sell recommendations for the company's exclusive portfolio or for customers of the particular firm.

    Skills You Need

    • Excel Modelling Skills: MTA charter holders, work on a lot of Excel sheet and also the models.
    • Analytical Skills: It gives them the ability to visualize, articulate, conceptualize or solve both complex and uncomplicated problems by making decisions that are sensible given the available information.
    • Communication Skills: They have to communicate with clients, investors, stockbrokers and many others.
    • Computer Skills: They may have to learn new software to create portfolios, analyse financial data, see trends, and make forecasts.
    • Mathematics Skills: MTA designation uses mathematical skills when estimating the value of financial securities.

    How To Pursue

    • School: Any subject combination taking Sience or Commerce or Humanities.
    • Graduation: Any Graduation Course / Bachelor of Commerce in Finance.
    • Post Graduation: 3 Level CMT Examination.
